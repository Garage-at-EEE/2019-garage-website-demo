An informal student self-run learning platform, Garage@EEE aims to cultivate entrepreneurial and innovative spirit by empowering students like ourselves to take ownership of our own learning, our projects, as well as our paths to self-discovery. It is also a place where we hope that students can bridge the gap between classroom and experiential learning. As Garage@EEE Ambassadors, we are passionate in what we do and we grow as a family. Stay tuned and catch us in action!
